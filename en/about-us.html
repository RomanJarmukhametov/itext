Our History

IText Translation Agency was founded in 2012 in Pavlodar, the Republic of Kazakhstan. Investments to open a translation agency amounted to 50 thousand tenges, which was used to rent an office and advertising.

Since our foundation, we have established partnerships with all major enterprises in the region and with many industrial companies in Kazakhstan, including in oil and gas production.

We have been growing steadily over the past seven years, increasing production from an average of 5,000 translations in 2015 to 93,000 in 2022.
